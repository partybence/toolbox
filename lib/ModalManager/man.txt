Helper for stacking bootstrap Modals.
Bootstrap's Modals are normally not stackable, their z-indexes are fixed by the stylesheet.
This class can register and keep track of open modals, dynamically setting their z-indexes to display on top of each-other.

Usage:
	> ModalManager.register(modalEl);
	where modalEl can be the HTML DOM modal element or the modal's id.
	Returns the bootstrap.Modal object.
	> ModalManager.deregister(modalEl);
	where modalEl can be the HTML DOM modal element, the modal's id or the bootstrap.Modal object.
	Removes event handlers that keep track of and manages the modal's position.
